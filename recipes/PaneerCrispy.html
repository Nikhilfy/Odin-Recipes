Paneer Crispy

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Cottage Cheese) 250 gm
  • Corn flour 2 tbsp
  • Fine flour or Maida (Refined flour) 2 tbsp
  • Ginger and garlic paste 2 tsp
  • Black pepper powder 1/2 tsp
  • Soy sauce 1 tbsp

For Sauce

  • Ginger 1 tbsp, finely chopped
  • Garlic 1 tbsp, finely chopped
  • Spring onion white 1, sliced
  • Capsicum 2, small size, chopped
  • Tomato ketchup 1 tbsp (Skip this step if you like it spicy)
  • Red chili sauce 1/2 tbsp
  • Salt as per taste
  • Vinegar 1/4 tbsp
  • Corn flour slurry 2 tbsp

Directions

  1. Take a mixing bowl, add corn flour, fine flour, ginger and garlic paste, soy sauce, water. Whisk all the ingredients to make a thick batter from it.
  2. Take a pan, add oil in it and heat it up, after heating of oil, take paneer cubes, dip them in a batter and coat batter on them well.
  3. Start frying coated paneer in hot oil.
  4. Fry them well and evenly from all the side and then keep them aside.
  5. In a pan, add chopped ginger and garlic, saute for 2 minutes and then add sliced spring onion white, slit green chilies, capsicum and black pepper powder. Mix all the things well and saute for 2 minutes.
  6. Then add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, red chili sauce and 1/2 cup of water, mix all things well.
  7. Add salt and corn flour slurry and stir it till the gravy thickens.
  8. Finally add fried paneer and vinegar, mix them well and serve it.
  9. Your crispy paneer recipe is ready, serve it hot.
